Guidelines - Sayuri

#1 - Bot Usage

1.1 - Do not abuse errors! Report them in my support server for resolution.
1.2 - The use of any GORE, NSFW content, or anything else that could harm Sayuri will result in a permanent ban.
1.3 - Flooding commands with the intent to crash or damage Sayuri will lead to immediate banishment.
1.4 - Attempting to exchange Sayuri's in-app currency for real-world goods, accounts, or servers will result in a permanent ban.
1.5 - For other bot developers, blatant copying of Sayuri's commands will lead to a ban.
1.6 - Any form of harassment or threats against users or staff within the Sayuri environment will not be tolerated and will result in a permanent ban.
1.7 - Engaging in or promoting cheating, exploiting, or any unethical behavior in the Sayuri ecosystem will result in an immediate ban.
1.8 - The use of automated scripts or bots to manipulate Sayuri’s functionality or economy is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.

#2 - Terms of Use and Discord Guidelines

To avoid issues, anyone who disrespects the Terms of Use and Guidelines of Discord will be permanently banned from Sayuri. This includes, but is not limited to, spamming, impersonation, or any form of misconduct.
